HI, WE ARE THE CHARITABLE INVESTMENTS CLUB
CHARITABLE INVOLVED EDUCATIONAL

Our club was made to help teach students about investment and better the community at the same time.

ABOUT THE CLUB

6 teenagers started this club with an interest in both investing, education, and charity. Together, they learned about the stock market and investments, and finally decided to put together everything they knew to help the community.

Together, we created an account worth $2,100. Our goal is to use what we’ve learned to grow that number through investing, and donate any and all of the profits we make to a good cause in the local area. Along the way, we will also conduct activities related to volunteering and giving back, which might entail some fundraising as well. COVID-19 brought unprecedented times, and we want to do our part in helping our community in anyway we can. It’ll be a great learning experience for us, in both investing and altruism. We are ready to everything we can… Are you ready to help us?
